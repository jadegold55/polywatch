# ADR-NNNN: Title

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by ADR-XXXX
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the problem? What constraints apply? What forces are at play?
Keep this short — a few sentences. Not a history essay.

## Decision

What was decided. One or two sentences.

## Alternatives considered

Bulleted list. For each: what it was, and why it wasn't picked.
The quality of an ADR is mostly in this section — if you can't name
the alternatives, you haven't actually made a decision.

## Consequences

What follows from this decision. Things that get easier, things that
get harder, new constraints, new obligations. Be honest about the
downsides.
